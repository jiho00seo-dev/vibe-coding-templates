# 📗 AI Portfolio Vibe Pack: Global Manual (English)

This manual guides non-developers through personalizing and deploying their **AI Portfolio** in minutes.

---

## 🏗️ Step 1: Preparation
1. **Your Pack**: `index_en.html`, `vibe-tokens.json`, etc.
2. **Cursor Editor**: Download at [cursor.com](https://cursor.com).
3. **Accounts**: Github and Cloudflare.

---

## 🌍 Step 2: Global Localization (Any Language)
The template is in English, but you can change it to **any language** (Korean, Spanish, etc.) effortlessly.

**Prompt for Cursor:**
> "I want to translate this entire website into [Your Language]. Please update all strings in `index_en.html` while ensuring the premium layout and Bento grid balance remain perfect."

---

## 🎨 Step 3: 1-Minute Personalization
Paste these prompts into Cursor's Chat (Ctrl+L):

> "Update the content with my info:
> - Name: [Your Name]
> - Role: [e.g., AI Research Engineer]
> - Slogan: [e.g., Designing the future of LLMs]
> - Stats: [e.g., 20+ projects, 5 years exp]"
